Siddhartha booklet

Graphic Design

× for: typography 1 × tools: adobe indesign, photoshop

Book design for Herman Hesse's 'Siddhartha'.

Showcased are the first few pages of the project. With this project, we applied our understanding of typography to a more traditional medium, the physical book. For the cover, I chose yellow and blue, which mean many different things in Buddhism depending on who you ask, but are nonetheless important colors in the faith.
