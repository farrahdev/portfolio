UX / UI Design

× tools: figma

For a course on information design, I created this interactive web experience to tell viewers the story of the January 6th riots on the White House. Ideally viewed on a tablet device in an educational environment, the purpose of this piece was to show the cause and effect of events in the buildup to the day of the riots. By displaying this information spatially, chronologically, and categorically, one can analyze and determine which states were most involved.
