A booklet to showcase the importance of typography.







For my Typography 1 final project, I learned about a typeface specimen, or a detailed study of one specific typeface. I chose gotham narrow, and styled the booklet with red and cyan. I was able to condense a lot of information in a small space, especially for the anatomy page. I also had the opportunity to utilize vertical type, a favorite element of mine.
