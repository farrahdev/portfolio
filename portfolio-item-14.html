3D letterform

Graphic Design

× for: typography 2 × tools: adobe indesign, poster board, scissors, tape

A 3D letterform, handcrafted and placed in common environments.







This project taught me to understand 3D and how lighting, angles, and scale affect a 3D object. Type was originally meant to be seen in the real world and has evolved to be on screens, so it was nice to pay homage to its traditional use. I also took into account the contrast that the K would have with the background, and created scenarios where the background harmonized with the letterform instead of purely distracting from it. This is an important concept even when designing on screens since the visual elements should work together instead of clash.
