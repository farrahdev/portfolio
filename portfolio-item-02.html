A brand redesign for an architecture magazine.











This project was given to simulate client to designer work practices. I was given a previous brand, Objekt Magazine, and a desired new concept, "spatial", and had to redesign the brand to match the concept. This was a great project to work with positive and negative space. There were also opportunities to bring in my knowledge of visual hierarchy, Gestalt principles, and grid applications.
