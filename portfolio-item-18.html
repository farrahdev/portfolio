terralove brand site

UI & Brand Design

× live preview (press Z to fit screen)
× for: interaction design 1
× tools: adobe illustrator, figma













This is a 2 part UI prototype and brand design for a business. My focus was to create a system of colors, typefaces, and visual elements that invoked the feeling of being environmentally conscious. For the logo, I used an illustration of a terrarium that I made in Adobe Illustrator. I then created a layout in Figma that covered all the necessary information for the shop's website. Then I used Figma's in-app image properties to manipulate images to fit the brand's purpose and aesthetic.
