keyboardbuilder

Product Design

× for: myself
× tools: figma, adobe illustrator

I love building keyboards, but I have trouble visualizing all the options, especially when there are so many keycap and case options on the market. So I use Figma to model my ideas before I buy the parts!

Everything is done in Figma, using auto layout and scaling the keys from the keyboard unit “u” (ex: 1u, 2.25u) to pixels (100px, 225 px).
