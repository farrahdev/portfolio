A system of colors, typefaces & design choices to represent a brand. I chose to create a fictional gaming family to represent my friends, inspired by brands like TSM, 100Thieves, and OfflineTV. I chose blue / yellow / black / white as the brand colors since they reminded me of electricity and modern technology. Monserrat was my typeface family choice because of its versatility in line weights, all while providing a clean look. The icon was made with the idea of abstracting the crown down to minimalist, simple shapes. I also gave all of the photos a duotone effect.

The website's layout was sketched on paper, but brought to life in Carrd, a website builder with customizable components.
