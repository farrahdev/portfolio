A redesign of Pinterest's mobile application. This group project focused on replacing the app's clunky UI with a more seamless exploratory experience.

















Working in a team of 3, I along with my group members utilized the design thinking process to empathize with current Pinterest users' concerns with the app, define our problem statement that will satisfy the interviewed users, ideate a variety of solutions, prototype the solutions in Figma, and test for user feedback in order to keep improving the design.

Our redesigned prototype introduced many new features, such as the manual refresh button, pin filtering, UI hierarchy overhaul, and improved search functions.
