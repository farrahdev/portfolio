virtualreality

Graphic Design

× for: brand and identity design
× tools: procreate, figma, adobe indesign

This is a project in which I asked myself to explore my interests and create a visual representation in a 12 page zine with 3 chapters.















In my zine, I explore the interconnecting of virtual + reality in my life as a lover of digital art, photography, design, and technology. The special concept I wanted to hone in on was the modularity of the printed booklet. The design is intended to encourage the user to flip the book once they got to the "end", and read "backwards" as they make their way back to page "1". I received a helpful feedback to include a visual indicator to make the direction more obvious - thus adding page numbers in my final version. It was an eventual challenge to prepare the design for printing, but doing so has taught me the correct process needed for booklet printing in InDesign.
