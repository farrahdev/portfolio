CSA event

Graphic Design

× for: NU CSA × tools: adobe photoshop

A brand design for a student event, the annual murder mystery dinner.

As a part of e-board, I was in charge of designing the advertisements for our event. We hosted a murder mystery dinner where we wrote a script and acted out parts of the play together. The theme that year was "Journey to the West Coast", a modern twist on the original Chinese novel it partially borrows its name from. I went with poppy retro colors to show that the event was meant to be whimsical and fun, not as scary as its name implied. I also used a lot of angles to mimic knife slashing.
