A booklet to showcase the importance of typography.

In this semester long project, I learned about InDesign's software basics, such as setting up master pages and automatic page numbering. I also learned about book design and client constraints, since each page had to have specific elements. As a bonus, the images were all taken from Wikimedia Commons, to teach us the importance of using license free images in our designs.
