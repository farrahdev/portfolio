CSA shirt

Graphic Design

× for: NU CSA × tools: adobe photoshop

A shirt design for Northeastern University's Chinese Student Association.

I was lucky enough to be on Northeastern University's Chinese Student Association executive board as their media and design specialist. One of my last projects on e-board was to create a shirt for members and admins alike to represent their involvement in the club. This design was illustrated in Photoshop. I was asked to create something simple, cute, and eye catching. I also needed to include the club's name and logo (which I also designed). I found the sleepy moon rabbit a motif that any college student in the club could relate to, and was satisfied with the contrast it had with the seal script logo and modern typeface.
